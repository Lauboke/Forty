Core Values

Here are some of our core values listed below.

Sustainable Education

Sustainable education is an approach to learning that seeks to promote environmental, social, and economic sustainability. It is an interdisciplinary approach that integrates knowledge and skills from various fields, including science, economics, social studies, and ethics. The goal is to provide learners with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to become responsible, engaged citizens who can contribute to building a more sustainable future.

Fun Fact About Education

The oldest university in the world is the University of Bologna, located in Italy. It was founded in 1088 and is still in operation today, making it over 900 years old! The University of Bologna is also credited with being the first university to use the term "universitas" to describe a community of scholars and students.

Quality Meals

Quality meals are meals that are nutritious, delicious, and prepared with care. They are meals that provide the body with the necessary nutrients to function properly, while also satisfying the taste buds. Quality meals are important for maintaining good health, preventing chronic diseases, and promoting overall well-being.

Decent Clothing

Decent clothing is an important aspect of supporting the basic needs of individuals and communities. Decent clothing refers to clothing that is clean, comfortable, and appropriate for the weather and occasion. It is clothing that promotes dignity, self-esteem, and confidence.

In a Nutshell

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the less fortunate members of our community. Our mission is to provide assistance and resources to those in need, with a focus on promoting dignity, self-sufficiency, and well-being.
